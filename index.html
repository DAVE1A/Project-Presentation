Benefits for Merchants

  • Drive more revenue

    Bolt’s optimized checkout flow recovers lost sales and boosts conversions from day one.

  • Access a network that converts

    With over 80M shoppers, the Bolt Network helps merchants instantly recognize returning customers—even if they've never shopped your site before.

  • Turn more first-timers into lifetime buyers

    Shoppers with Bolt Accounts are 15% more likely to make a repeat purchase than guests.

  • Boost shopper security and enhance account protection

    Provide customers more secure logins using passwordless OTP and account takeover protection (ATO). And our double opt-in model ensures shoppers stay in control of their information.

  • Offload PCI compliance checkout responsibility

    By saving shipping and payment information within Bolt (instead of store accounts), Bolt ensures that all PII is tokenized and securely stored.

Benefits for Shoppers

  • Saved address and payment information

    Offer customers a way to save payment information and complete purchases with a one-click checkout.

  • Passwordless login

    Let shoppers use a secure, OTP without needing to remember usernames or passwords.

  • Faster checkout across retailers

    One-click, logged-in checkout experience across all retailers in the Bolt network.

  • Centralized security & account protection

    Store data in a single location instead of across multiple store accounts.

  • Easy access to order tracking and reordering

    Reorder or buy new products easily using saved Bolt account info.
